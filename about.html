About Frida and Willie

Frida and Willie are experienced safari guides with a deep-rooted love for Botswana’s landscapes, wildlife, and cultural heritage.

Frida

With over 15 years of guiding experience, Frida specializes in the Okavango Delta and conservation efforts.

Willie

A native of Botswana, Willie’s expertise spans the Central Kalahari and Chobe National Park.
